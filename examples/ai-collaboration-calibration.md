# Example: ai-collaboration-calibration

## Use When

You have a fuzzy product idea, but the real problem, user, or decision criteria are not clear yet.

## Prompt

```text
$ai-collaboration-calibration

我想做一个 AI 原生时间管理工具，但现在越想越复杂：
既想自动排日程，又想做目标管理，还想分析我的工作模式。
先别帮我写 PRD，帮我判断这个问题是不是想偏了。
```

## Expected Output Shape

- Restates the current product idea in one sentence.
- Identifies hidden assumptions.
- Separates user problem, product promise, and implementation temptation.
- Challenges the most fragile assumption first.
- Gives a recommended framing, not a full PRD.
- Ends with one or a few concrete next questions.

## Good Follow-Up

```text
按你刚才的判断，把这个问题重新定义成一个可验证的产品假设。
```
