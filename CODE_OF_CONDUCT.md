# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple contribution standard:

- Be respectful and specific.
- Critique ideas, workflows, and examples rather than people.
- Do not include private company data, customer data, credentials, or confidential context.
- Keep discussions focused on improving reusable AI PM workflows.
- Assume public visibility for every issue, PR, and example.

Maintainers may remove comments, issues, PRs, or examples that are abusive, off-topic, spammy, or unsafe to publish.

If you need to report a concern, open a private channel with the maintainer instead of posting sensitive details publicly.
